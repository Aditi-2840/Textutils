About Textutils


Textutils is a simple testing website made by Aditi Kumari using Django and Python as a backend and HTML, CSS and Bootstrap as a frontend.


Textutils helps you to get your text and do some changes as per your choice such as :- Remove all the Punctuations, Capitalize your letters, Remove all the spaces from your text and Remove all the extra line that you have given by mistakes.
